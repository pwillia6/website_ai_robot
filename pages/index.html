ACMS brings non-professional musicians from Sydney, Canberra and Brisbane to play Chamber Music for the enjoyment that such playing brings. Whether you're a string, wind, keyboard, or vocal performer, take advantage of our friendly society that facilitates the organization of ensembles by providing curated music, comfortable venues, and contacts with like-minded musicians.

Quick Impact

Why join ACMS?

  • Instant Contacts: Search our national database of over 380 active chamber musicians categorized by skill & instrument.
  • Extensive Music Library: Borrow complete part sets for your next private session or public playing day.
  • Bi-Monthly Playing Days: Professional tutoring provided for grouped ensembles.

Who We Are

We are a passionate non-profit organization of committed non-professional chamber music players. Our aim is to further amateur music-making and create lifetime friendships through shared musical growth.

What We Do

We arrange regular Playing Days, connect members using verified self-grading guides, compile custom ensemble rosters, maintain an extensive catalog, and host an epic 3-day annual camp.

Benefits of Joining

Gain entry to playing pools, borrow historical parts for free, receive tutoring from world-class professionals, and perform before supportive, intimate audiences at beautiful local venues.
