The Highlight of Our Year

Frensham Music Camp — Mittagong 2026

2 – 5 October 2026

After several wonderful years, we have bid farewell to Bathurst and moved the annual 3-day music camp to the delightful Frensham School at Mittagong. Located just over an hour's drive from Sydney, Frensham welcomes ACMS to participate in its rich musical legacy. Pianos are abundant, food and student accommodation are adjacent, and transit stress is non-existent.
