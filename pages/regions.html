Sydney Region

ACMS is based in Sydney, with about 300 keen chamber musicians. We hold regular playing days, concerts, and an annual music camp. Membership is $28 per year ($23 concession).

Brisbane Region

Queensland Amateur Chamber Music Society (QACMS) amalgamated with ACMS in 2009. We are always keen to attract more players. For enquiries, contact Sylvia MacDonald via email: qacms@optusnet.com.au

Canberra Region

ACMS events in the ACT are organised locally by Canberra Chamber Music Players, usually at the ANU School of Music. We offer prospective new members a free introductory playing day.

Adelaide (ACMPSA)

The Associated Chamber Music Players of South Australia Inc. (ACMPSA) is an affiliated society for chamber music enthusiasts in SA. They arrange playing days three times a year.
