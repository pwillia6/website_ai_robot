Member Benefits

The items listed below are some of the many benefits of membership of ACMS.

Membership Listing

Members have access to a membership database with contact details to help you organize chamber music groups with others. It's automatically updated, and you can manage your own profile.

Newsletter

Our President's Report is e-mailed to members four times a year, providing details of upcoming events and other important society information.

Playing Days

Participate in our bi-monthly playing days (from March, excluding January) in Sydney, and at various times throughout the year in Canberra and Brisbane.

Annual Music Camp

Join our popular annual Music Camp, typically a 3-day residential event offering intensive playing and social opportunities.

Kirribilli Concerts

Gain invaluable performance experience with opportunities to play in chamber music concerts before a small, appreciative audience regularly throughout the year.

Music Library

Access our extensive music library for your practice and performance needs. Certain borrowing rules apply to ensure availability for events.
