Library Policies

ACMS maintains an extensive archive of classical music sets. Members can borrow parts between playing days.

Returns Deadline:

Borrowers must return sheets five (5) days prior to any playing day or at the next playing day.

Zero Marking Policy:

No pencil, pen, fingerings, or bowings are permitted on library catalog originals. Damaged sheets require total original set reimbursement.

Registered mail:

Borrowings are mailed via trackable registered mail (at borrower's expense). Returns must follow matching precautions.

Online copies:

If an item is out of copyright, it can be scanned and made available in the online library for members.
