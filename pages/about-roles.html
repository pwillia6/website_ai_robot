Committee Roles & Duties

How the society operates. If you are interested in volunteering, contact the Secretary inside the Members Area.

Elected Core Roles

  • President: Chairs committee meetings, serves as the public face of the society, prepares newsletters and reports.
  • Secretary: Handles minutes of meetings, manages record keeping, and logs external correspondence.
  • Treasurer: Handles annual subscriptions, balances bank transactions, and logs expenses.

Co-opted Specialized Roles

  • Administrator: Coordinates registrations, Google Admin consoles, and keeps databases synchronized.
  • Camp Organiser: Establishes venue contracts, manages accommodation blocks, and processes brochures.
  • Group Organiser: Assembles play-groups, pairs similar levels, and evaluates balance of wind/string elements.
  • Librarian: Oversees postage of physical music parts and verifies complete sets on returns.
